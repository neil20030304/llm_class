"Plan my trip to Paris next week"

🌤️ Weather Summary

Paris will experience mild spring weather with temperatures ranging from 15-22°C (59-72°F). Expect partly cloudy skies with a 30% chance of rain mid-week.

🎒 Packing List

  • Light jacket or cardigan
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Compact umbrella
  • Layers (t-shirts + sweater)
  • Sunglasses & sunscreen
  • Light scarf for evenings

🎯 Activity Ideas

  • Stroll through Luxembourg Gardens
  • Seine River evening cruise
  • Louvre Museum (rainy day backup)
  • Montmartre neighborhood walk
  • Outdoor café hopping
  • Picnic at Champ de Mars
