PRAYER
The Chaplain, the Reverend Margaret
Grun Kibben, offered the following
prayer:
Holy and eternal God, we commend
to You this past year and pray Your
blessing over all our efforts.
Should we be consumed with pride
over our accomplishments, humble us,
for nothing we do is outside of Your
sovereign plan. Should we be overwhelmed with frustration, believing
our best attempts have been thwarted,
raise us up, for all things, when done in
good faith, will find reward in the fullness of time.
All of this—our successes, our failures, our achievements, our losses—we
commend to You. For any glory we
gain is like the flowers of the field, and
any defeat we suffer is like the grass
that withers, but Your Word, O Lord,
endures forever.

Speak then Your final Word over the
first half of the 119th Congress, that we
would know the might of Your righteousness and the mercy of Your reward.
All this we ask by Your endless grace
and in Your matchless name.
Amen.
