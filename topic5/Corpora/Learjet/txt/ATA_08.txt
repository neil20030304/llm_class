                      LEVELING - DESCRIPTION AND OPERATION

1.      General
        A. Aircraft leveling is accomplished by using a plumb bob clip and level-
           ing plate located in the upper and lower tailcone area immediately aft
           of frame 26A (Sta. 492. 55) at BL 0. A string with plumb bob attached
           is suspended down from the inboard side of the clip so that the plumb
           bob point extends into the hole of the leveling plate. The aircraft is
           considered level when the plumb bob· extends -into, but does not touch
           the sides of the hole.
